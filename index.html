Delhi Public School, NTPC Farakka

Delhi Public School NTPC Farakka location is at Farakka, Murshidabad. It is a CBSE school, with a team of 36 dedicated and professional faculties, which are here to ensure that the children get the most from their education.
In 1993, Delhi Public School was launched. English is the primary medium of instruction for this school and the student teacher ratio is 29:1. The school takes pride of its excellent teaching methodology. Classes from 1 to 12 run in this school. This school has approximately 1048 as student strength.
